# Cookies

* **Session Cookies**
  * Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
  * Used to maintain a user session (e.g., keeping you logged in while browsing a site).
* **Persistent Cookies**
  * Stored on your device even after closing the browser, until they expire.
  * Used for remembering login info, preferences, or tracking behavior over time.
* **Strictly Necessary Cookies**
  * Required for the website to function properly.
  * Examples: shopping cart contents, login authentication.
* **Performance/Analytics Cookies**
  * Collect info about how users interact with the site.
  * Examples: Google Analytics tracking.
* **Functionality Cookies**
  * Remember choices and preferences.
  * Example: language or theme settings.
* **Targeting/Advertising Cookies**
  * Track browsing habits to show relevant ads.
  * Often used by third-party advertisers.
